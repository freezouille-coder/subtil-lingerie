Watercolor botanical composition for a website background banner, evoking the brand spirit of "Subtil Lingerie" (which uses watercolor florals in its logo).

Composition: large painterly watercolor florals — magnolia blossoms in dusty mauve, peony heads in deep aubergine, with delicate stems, leaves, and a few golden champagne accents. The flowers cluster on the LEFT side of the frame, with generous CREAM/IVORY empty space on the RIGHT side (this empty space is critical: it will host overlay text and a form on the website).

Mood: feminine, refined, airy, hand-painted, never busy.
Style: traditional watercolor on cold-pressed paper, visible brush strokes, soft paint bleeds, slight paper grain texture, NOT digital, NOT photographic, NOT cartoon. Reminiscent of fashion illustrator Inslee Fariss or watercolor artist Tara Sweeney.
Color palette: aubergine #6B3E5E, dusty mauve #B898AC, champagne gold #C9A961, cream #FAF6F1 background.
Composition: very wide horizontal banner 3:1, flowers anchored left/lower-left, lots of negative space on the right two-thirds.

Strict constraints:
- NO figures, NO bodies, NO faces, NO people
- NO text, NO logos, NO writing of any kind
- NO photographic realism — must look like a hand-painted watercolor
- NO heavy saturation, keep it powdery and soft
- The right two-thirds must remain mostly empty cream background for layout overlay
